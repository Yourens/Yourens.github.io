    How to make a presentation for your paper as a non-native English speaker

    2 minute read

    This post is all about my personal experience. Take it critically with your own perspective.

    If you publish a paper on a conference, then you have to make a presentation in English to introduce your work. This presentation is about how others feels about your work and how others feels about you in your scientific community.
    So it’s very important to make a good preparation for your presentation. However, for our non-native English speaker, making a long presentation is never an easy task. I share some of my experience about preparing presentation for your paper, for others but also for my amnesia.

    1. Pick up some most important points, creative opinion in your paper that you mostly want to share in your presentation. (two hours)

    Those points might be some story in your paper, some mechanism you are proud of, or even some new founding in your experiments. Those points are what you must share to others.

    1. Give a logic of your presentation. (Half day)

    The logic in your presentation is about the organization of your slides. Imaging all audience know nothing about your work even background about your work, how can you make them feel interest, and, how to introduce your work clearly?

    So, in this stage, you think about a logic, which including what’s the problem you want to solve, why you are doing this work, why it’s useful and how difficult it is, what’s your solution for the difficulties, and most important, the connections between them.

    This logic must concatenate all points from the first stage (Because they are the most thing you want to present, right?). After this stage, you already have a framework of your presentation. In your logic, there should be no flaw like use concepts before its definition or anything suddenly jumped out. All slides go smoothly.

    Also, you need to consider about times you spend. Usually a talk is comprised of a story, previous works and your own contribution. Remember emphasize your work rather than talk about background too much.

    1. Design your presentation frame by frame with a notebook. (one to two days)

    With a logic, now you can design your slides. In this stage, paints the slides frame by frame on a notebook.

    Inside one frame, you have to design a detailed layout including text and pictures you want to put in this frame. What’s the content on this page and can you illustrate it with a picture? If so, what’s this picture would looks like.

    Between the frame, think about conjunctive words, how to move to next frame after this frame, are those two frames relative? If not, why just put it to other places.

    Since author is a non-native English speaker, the content in the frame need to shape and polish.

    1. Write the script and read it again and again.(one to two days)

    After stage three, now you have a detail slide. However, this usually is not the final version. You should write a script about your presentation and practice it again and again. Once you find any problem of your slide or script like logic problem or text in slide is not accurate enough, fix it. You can always improve your presentation, the way to improve it is practice it again and again.

    1. At last, write your slides with power point or keynote. Then practice it with time control. (until your presentation)

    At this stage, you might still want to improve your work. But remember, don’t update your slides in logic if necessary. This will waste your practice time you spend.
